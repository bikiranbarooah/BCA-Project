About Eklavya Schooting Sports Academy Website

Shooting is a sport that is open to people of all ages and it is not unusual to find talented teenagers competing against people old enough to be their grandparents at some local events. It requires good physical and psychological conditioning. As shooters strive to achieve technical perfection, a steady hand and nerves of steel are a must for success. Earlier the shooters from Assam had to participate in shooting competitions outside the state to qualify for National Shooting Championship Competitions due to lack of infrastructure in the state. Assam Rifle and Shooting Association, (ARSA), has conducted various State Level, Pre National Level, National Level and International Level Shooting Championships at this facility. They are also providing regular coaching sessions at the facility to renowned, aspiring and budding shooters of the state.

They have conducted various shooting orientation camps for the grassroots development of the sport in Assam. In May 2020, a private shooting range was established by a society named Eklavya Shooting Sports Academy at the Beltola Tiniali, Guwahati. Eklavya Shooting Sports Academy provides regular coaching sessions in the 10 meter Air Pistol and Air Rifle disciplines to the participants.They have conducted shooting coaching camps for students of schools and colleges at Guwahati. Special shooting camps have also been conducted to engage the under privileged children, Senior Citizens as well as differently abled persons of the state. We are a team of traineer with an hope of helping everyone to register online shooting sports. We set this website with a vision of visiting and look at various shooting activities, information. We have images showcasing different activities throughout the year. You can also register online for membership of the academy and admission to join courses.
